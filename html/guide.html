Measure and Evaluate Progress

Now that you have identified some actions to address your EHR goals, you may want to consider what tools you can use to measure your progress.

Compare

Identify baseline rates for preventive services, such as vaccination, within the individual practice. Compare these rates to benchmark rates, on a local, regional, state, or national level. As a starting point for comparison, look to the Healthy People 2020 goals and CDC data and statistics.

Assess

The practice or Integrated Delivery System (IDS) can establish Clinical Quality Measures (CQMs), called Meaningful Use Quality Measures in Epic, to set and assess performance against quality goals.

  • Quality Dashboards are used to view the entire patient population
  • Snapshot is used to view an individual patient
  • IDSs are able to add new CQMs or to modify the existing CQMs
  • IDSs may have an approval process to establish and modify the CQMs

By reviewing measures over time, practices can evaluate the effectiveness of their quality improvement activities like improving patient education, use of reminders to reduce gaps in vaccinations, or recalling patients for additional treatments.

CQMs reporting can be used as a Patient List to bring patients back into the practice to help close gaps in care.
