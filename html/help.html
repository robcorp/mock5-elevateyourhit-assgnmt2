FAQs

Q: How do I change my login information?

A: You can change your login information by accessing and editing your profile.

Q: How do I add additional EHRs to use with this guide?

A: You can add additional EHRs by editing your profile in the “Please choose one or more Electronic Health Record” section. The EHR will then become an active selection the next time you log in.
